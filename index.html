The Story
Available for Freelance

Your vision.
My craft.

I am Ahmed Mobasher, a Computer Science and Engineering student with a mind that loves code and an eye that refuses to settle for anything ordinary. My story did not start in a classroom. It started in a dark room, with a timeline on the screen and a story that needed telling.

In 2020, I began freelancing — picking up video editing projects, staying up late to find the perfect cut, chasing the frame that made a story breathe. That obsession quietly grew into graphics design, where every pixel became a decision and every layout a statement. I learned to think in color, type, and composition.

Now I am channeling all of that visual instinct into web design and app development, building digital experiences that do not just function but actually feel like something. And when I step away from screens entirely, I am deep into robotics — because sometimes you need to build the future with your hands first before you code it into existence.
